Lasagna

A Delicious Homemade Lasagna that will have your family begging for seconds.

Ingredients

  • Pasta Sheets
  • 500g Beef Mince
  • 2 Eggs
  • Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F | 180°C.
  2. Spoon about 1 cup of meat sauce on the base of a 9x13-inch baking dish, then cover with lasagna sheets. (Trim sheets to fit over the meat if needed.)
  3. Layer with 2 cups of meat sauce (or enough to cover pasta), 1 cup of white sauce and half of the mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers (leaving the remaining cheese for the top).
  4. Pour the remaining meat sauce and white sauce over the last layer of lasagna sheets and top with the remaining mozzarella cheese. Bake for 25 minutes or until golden and bubbling.
